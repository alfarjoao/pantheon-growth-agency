Physical Store + Online

Physical location drives walk-ins. Online store drives shipping orders. But they cannibalize each other. Local SEO competes with paid ads for the same customer. Your CAC is 2.8× higher than pure-play competitors.

CAC Multiplier 2.8–3.5×

You get calls. You get form fills. GMB drives inquiries. But 55–70% of qualified leads never book. Whether you're ranking #1 or just starting SEO, leads leak between touchpoints. Phone → Website → Booking is a black hole.
